# Impact Taxonomy

The impact taxonomy is used to categorize the impact of a project or initiative. It is used to help users understand the potential impact of a project or initiative. Impacts are either measured or implicit. Measured impacts are those that can be quantified, while implicit impacts are those that are not easily quantifiable.
